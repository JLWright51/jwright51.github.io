Process & Timeline

How It Works

  • Stage One

    Discovery

    The first part of creating or updating your website is to find out what your unique goals are for your business online. We offer free prototyping during this phase without any commitment. (Limited time offer) During this time we pin point objectives for the website and find out the best way to achieve your goals online.

  • Stage Two

    Rough Draft

    During this phase we create a rough draft of the website, setting up the layout and overall theme of the website while creating content and taking location, employee, and product photos if necessary.

  • Stage Three

    First Build

    At this point we finish up the website and its content. Photos should be edited and in place and content up. The first build brings the website to a usable point with all of the functions and pages working properly.

  • Stage Four

    Revisions

    When making revisions we look over the website and double check that everything, both style and function, meets your needs. We then make changes as necessary until we're sure that your website is perfect.

  • Stage Five

    Launch

    Once all revisions have been finalized, the website is ready to launch. We then host and manage your website, along with taking care of any additional online needs you may have, such as ad campaigns or ongoing analytics.

Search Engine Optimization

Where your website shows up in a search is very important.
More visibility - more customers!

  • Keywords
  • Content
  • Pay Per Click

Keywords are an important part of Search Engine Optimization. Keywords help link your website to potential customers who are searching for phrases related to your business.

Keywords can not only help your business reach new customers by linking your website to what they search for, but it can also help your website appear higher on internet search results so that potential customers see your business before competitors.

Having relevant content on your website also helps your site to rank higher in search engine results. For example, a criminal attorney may become ranked higher by a search engine simply because they included a page explaining miranda rights and it generated web traffic for their site.


One of the most important parts of Search Engine Optimization is having content that people are looking for. Even if the people reading the information do not become clients, most search engines take traffic into account when deciding where to rank a website on their results page.

Pay Per Click advertising can help increase your business's online visibility. Google, for example, lists their advertisements above the regular search results. Pay Per Click, or PPC, allows you to put up ads that gain visibility while only charging you when someone clicks on the ad.



Analytics

We can track and analyze web traffic coming to your site and use this information to find ways to improve traffic inflow and retention. We use various top of the line tools and software to help monitor visitors to your website in order to find out what brings them to you and, just as importantly, what can be done to encourage them to stay.

Beyond just counting the number of page views we also see the amount of time spent on the website and which pages are doing the most to help your business. We can then follow up on this by playing to the strengths we find while working to improve any areas that need work helping you to turn more potential customers into loyal clients.
