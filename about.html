Who We Are and What We Do

We are a small web design company serving all of north Mississippi. We are students at the University of Mississippi with various design skills who have come together to build something during our time in college and help our community while also offsetting some of our education expenses. As students with unique skillsets we have the ability to satisfy the needs of our clients while keeping costs below that of our competitors. We handle everything your business needs to succeed online. From online ad campaigns and content creation, to designing and hosting your website, we're here to help you reach your goals. Beyond the creation of your website, we want to partner with you to achieve long term success for your business.


We want to get to know your company and its goals, so that we can help your business reach its full potential.

Process & Timeline

How It Works

  • Stage One

    Discovery

    The first part of creating or updating your website is to find out what your unique goals are for your business online. We offer free prototyping during this phase without any commitment. (Limited time offer) During this time we pin point objectives for the website and find out the best way to achieve your goals online.

  • Stage Two

    Rough Draft

    During this phase we create a rough draft of the website, setting up the layout and overall theme of the website while creating content and taking location, employee, and product photos if necessary.

  • Stage Three

    First Build

    At this point we finish up the website and its content. Photos should be edited and in place and content up. The first build brings the website to a usable point with all of the functions and pages working properly.

  • Stage Four

    Revisions

    When making revisions we look over the website and double check that everything, both style and function, meets your needs. We then make changes as necessary until we're sure that your website is perfect.

  • Stage Five

    Launch

    Once all revisions have been finalized, the website is ready to launch. We then host and manage your website, along with taking care of any additional online needs you may have, such as ad campaigns or ongoing analytics.
