Who we are

We are a design firm based in Oxford, Mississippi. We specialize in website creation and online marketing. With years of experience in programming and design we provide quality service to our clients.

What we do

We want to be your IT department. We not only build websites, we offer ongoing analytics and updates to monitor and improve the utility of your firm and see how we can improve. This continual optimization ensures that you get the most out of your website and online marketing.

Why choose us

A well made website is an increasingly essential part of modern commerce with studies showing that more than 85% of consumers use the internet to search for businesses. We at Launchpoint Designs take the time to get to know your business and help you discover exactly what your organization needs to succeed.

Web Design

A website is an online brochure for your business, made for the purpose of generating sales leads and demonstrating to customers why they should choose your business above a competitor's. We provide quality websites, hand crafted to fit your business's needs.

Online Marketing

Having an online presence is of the utmost importance and becoming a larger part of marketing every day. We help your business grow, utilizing everything from online ad campaigns to social media posts.

Search Engine Optimization

Search rankings are an important part of how potential customers discover your business. Search engine optimization, or SEO, is the process of designing a website so that it appears higher on the list when using search engines such as Google and Bing.

Web Hosting

We can provide hosting and maintenance for your website, keeping things running smoothly so you can focus on running your business.

Our Services Include:

Responsive Design

Having a website that works well on different devices is important. Our websites are made to function on everything from smartphones to desktops.

Ad Campaigns

We can provide online ad campaigns, tailored to fit your target audience.

Content Marketing

Running a business takes time. With our content marketing service, we take the stress out of managing your online media presence by creating and managing social media posts for you.

Analytics

We offer analytics services for your website, helping you track not only visitors and traffic, but what parts of your website people use the most and which parts could be reworked to provide better results.

Pay Per Click

This is a type of ad campaign that allows you to advertise, but only pay when you see results. Rather than paying for each ad you place, you pay only when a potential customer uses the ad to visit your website.

Information Technology

Let us be the IT department for your small business. We can handle everything from search engine optimization to running online inventory and ad campaigns and monitoring email.
